ABOUT US

We are a company of promotion and representation of high performance athletes, specialized in soccer players, with emphasis on the development and consolidation of players and new promises, we act in South America, Central America, Mexico, USA, Asia and Europe.

We promote the professional career of our athletes, as well as, we link the image of our players with sponsoring brands and we have a Network of Agents in all continents, providing excellent opportunities for our represented.

Our History

BM Soccer sports advisory and Marketing was created in 2004, by Brazilian Player Agent Júnior Tosta (Juninho), with the idea of being a professional alternative for the promotion of professional players and an opportunity for promising young soccer players, providing administrative, fiscal, legal and psychological support to athletes. The company has a department of visions that is in charge of Davi Cortes (former professional soccer player of the club Santos of Brazil, UDG-Méx, Verdy Kawasaky and Brazilian team) and Everaldo Barbosa (former professional soccer player of the Club Jaguares de Chiapas-Méx, Necaxa-Mex, Guarani, Atlético Paranaense, Marilia, Santa Cruz, Ituano, Mogi Mirim) as well as Associated Agents, among them, Elder Granja (former Professional Soccer player of the International Club where he was world champion, Vasco da Gama, Palmeiras, Atlético Mineiro, Sport, Portuguesa, Corinthias-AL).
